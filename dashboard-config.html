Configure the School Dashboard

Sign in with your admin Google account to configure the dashboard.

🎨 Canva

Open your Canva presentation, click Share → More → Embed, and paste the URL.
The URL ends in /view?embed. Canva handles its own auto-play loop — the dashboard does not reload the iframe on a timer, so the presentation plays continuously without interruption.
Leave blank to show the school logo instead.

🕐 Schedules

📊 House Scores Sheet

Open your scores sheet, File → Share → Publish to web, choose Comma-separated values (.csv), click Publish, and paste the generated URL.
Expected structure: row 1 = house names (headers), row 2 = scores. Columns A=Accomodore, B=Callidus, C=Princeps, D=Vevaios.
The dashboard fetches this every 5 minutes.
